How to play the game?

Drummy will hear a simple song with a percussion instrument that he will have to play. If the notes are played in the correct order drummy has won that level and goes on to the next.

To move Drummy

To hit

Drummy can destroy obstacles with his powerful drumstick by using the B key on the keyboard.

To play percussion instruments

Drummy can use these buttons to play different notes of the percussion instruments.

Gameplay

To save your friend you need to play the notes in the right order. When you reach your friend the notes will play and you'll have to repeat them.

Maximum time

The maximum time to finish all the levels will be of 10 minutes. If the player cannot finish before this time then it'll be game over.
