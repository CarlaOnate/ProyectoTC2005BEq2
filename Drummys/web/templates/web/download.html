How to play the game?

Drummy will have to find his friends and save them. Drummy has to find them somewhere inside the level and find out how they got hurt.

To move Drummy

To hit

Drummy can destroy obstacles with his powerful drumstick by using the J key on the keyboard.

To interact with the environment

Drummy can use space to see the messages from his teammates or instructions from the sign.

Gameplay

To save your friend you need to play the notes in the right order. When you reach your friend the notes will play and you'll have to repeat them.

Maximum time

The maximum time to finish all the levels will be of 10 minutes. If the player cannot finish before this time then it'll be game over.
