Our Goal

This game has the goal to entertain the player while teaching them about percussion instruments, as well as raising awarness on the health issues that playing a percussion instrument can have.

Gameplay

Drummy is a percussionst player who has to use his drummer's skills to save his friends. While doing so, Drummy can teach you tips to prevent injuries while learning about percussion instruments.

Learning Process

This game teaches the player some of the main perscussion instruments. It also raises awarness on the injuries that can occur from playing percussion instruments and the main steps to avoid them.
